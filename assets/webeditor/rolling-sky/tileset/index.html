Rolling Sky Tileset

The tileset with debug numbers shows you the Tile IDs on the top of left of the tile. The without debug numbers version, as you can guess, does not have those numbers.

You can use this tileset in any content, but make sure to link back to this website so that others can also use the tileset. You may not claim that the tileset is your own.

Note that I have added many anti theft features. I spent over a month and a half on this, so the least you can do is credit me.
